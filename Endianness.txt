Little Endianness
 - Values of least importance will be ordered first
 - in a value of 12345678h the values would be stored at these offsets
	0: 78
	1: 56
	2: 34
	3: 12

Big Endianness
 - Values of most importance will be ordered first
 - In a value of 12345678h the values wouild be stored in these offsets
	0: 12
	1: 34
	2: 56
	3: 78
